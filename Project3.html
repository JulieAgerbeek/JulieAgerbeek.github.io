3D Treehouse

Making the treehouse

For a subject in my first year of studying Creative Media and Game Development, we had to create a 3D model based on the concept of a treehouse. I struggled with creating (low enough poly) organic shapes and models. I also struggled with working with Maya, which was the engine the school wanted us to use for creating the 3D model. Eventually though, I made a low enough poly 3D model based on the idea of Jack and the Beanstalk. The tree is a big beanstalk with multiple small branches and the house is based on a medieval type wooden beamed house.

The house is fitted with windows, supporting beams, small lanterns at the front of the house, a chimney and even a small axe in an axeholder at the back of the house. In the whole 3D model there are no Ngons and the house model should prove easy to texture. I made the beanstalk using vertex. It provided low poly organic shapes that looked like a beanstalk.
